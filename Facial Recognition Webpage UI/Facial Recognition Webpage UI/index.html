What is EchoGate.

EchoGate is an innovative facial recognition security system designed to give homeowners unparalleled peace of mind. By maintaining a personalized database of familiar faces, EchoGate can instantly identify visitors at your doorstep. When an unknown face is detected, the software immediately alerts the homeowner, sending a picture of the individual directly to their phone, and starting a live feed recording. Homeowners are then given the choice to interact with the visitor remotely or escalate the situation by notifying local authorities, complete with the address and a snapshot of the unknown person. The system activates only when motion is detected, optimizing efficiency while ensuring that every potential threat is analyzed in real-time.

Beyond simple notifications, EchoGate empowers homeowners to take immediate action in potential security situations. If an unrecognized individual fails to comply with the system’s prompt to approach the camera, the software triggers an emergency response, allowing the homeowner to communicate directly or contact the police instantly. By combining advanced facial recognition with direct intervention capabilities, EchoGate not only protects homes but also delivers confidence, control, and rapid response - transforming traditional home security into a smarter, safer, and more proactive experience.
